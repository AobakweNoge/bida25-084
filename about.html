About Us

we believe every home, business, and farm deserves to be a safe, pest-free environment. Founded on a commitment to quality, integrity, and results, we have grown into a trusted pest control partner for clients across the region.

MPN Holdings is a pest control company founded in 2000 in Botswana that promotes safe living environments, eco-responsible treatment and professional pest management for the benefit of every household and business.
We work across the areas where it counts most — we support residential, commercial, agricultural and civic properties, help eliminate infestations, develop long-term prevention plans, and advise and train property owners who lack the knowledge to handle pest threats on their own.
For over 20 years, we have helped over 5,000 homes, businesses and farms reclaim their spaces across multiple pest categories including rodents, flying insects, crawling pests and agricultural infestations. Our goal is to protect and preserve the environments people live and work in by providing high-quality pest control that equips properties with lasting, reliable defence against re-infestation. We also offer various prevention programmes and property assessments to help owners and managers further safeguard their spaces and maintain pest-free environments long after treatment.

Rodent Control

Rodent pests are mammals—most commonly rats, mice, and squirrels—characterized by continuously growing, sharp incisors used for gnawing

Rats, mice, and squirrels. We locate entry points, deploy targeted traps, and seal gaps permanently.

  • Flying Pest Control

    Flying pests are insects that use wings to fly, often causing annoyance, health hazards, or structural damage to homes.

    Mosquitoes, wasps, hornets, and flies. Treatment plans covering both indoor harborage and outdoor breeding sites.

  • Crawling Pest Control

    Crawling pests are insects and arthropods that scuttle, walk, or creep rather than fly, often invading homes for shelter, moisture, or food.

    Cockroaches, ants, bed bugs, and termites. We eliminate colonies at the source and prevent re-infestation.
